Approach
01 / 04
Start with constraints, not ideas

Most people start with a solution. I start with what's actually blocking progress — resources, regulations, adoption barriers. The constraint is usually the product insight.

02 / 04
Ship early, iterate in public

82 children in the Autest pilot. 33 participants in the Yale EMA study. A live metro system. Real validation beats internal conviction every time.

03 / 04
Optimize for adoption, not elegance

Autest is still used in 5 special schools and 3 NGOs — because it was designed for the actual user context, not for a demo. Adoption is the only metric that matters.

04 / 04
Evidence over ego

I build systems that outlast my involvement. The goal is a repeatable process that scales after I leave — not credit for being in the room.
