Meet Aranyo

Aranyo Ray graduated from Yale University with a degree in Computer Science and Psychology. He has worked across consulting, regtech, agritech, behavioral science, AI, and finance. At Yale, he conducted research with neurodiverse children, worked in psychology labs at the School of Medicine, earned a fellowship at the Wu Tsai Institute, and served as a teaching assistant for computer science courses. His experience spans from building AI-powered safety tools at Komal to winning national hackathons and securing government grants for mobility infrastructure. Today, as CTO of Komal, he applies everything he has learned to make the internet safer for children.

"I see myself in curious students and want them to flourish."

What is Komal?

Komal is a child internet safety and guidance app powered by ethical AI. It is built to help kids explore the internet safely, providing age-appropriate guidance and nudges that support learning, creativity, and research without exposing them to harmful content.
