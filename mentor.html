How It Works

A structured, high-touch process from first conversation to final decision

01

Initial Assessment

Deep-dive conversation to understand the student's interests, strengths, academic standing, and goals — before recommending a single thing.

02

Holistic Profile Building

Build from the bottom up. Identify and encourage the student's best self — academics, extracurriculars, research, leadership, and personal narrative woven together.

03

Strategic Direction

Specific guidance on tailoring pursuits to build a uniquely competitive profile. Not cookie-cutter: every student gets a different playbook.

04

Timeline & Execution

An efficient plan bringing all ingredients together: Personal Growth → Fit → Competitive Fit → Right College → Best Application.

05

Application Strategy

Curriculum planning, standardized testing, letters of recommendation, college essays, interviews, Early vs. Regular decisions, and final selection from offers.

06

Dedicated Access

I take a small number of students at a time. Each one gets direct, ongoing access to my time and expertise — regular calls, quick-turnaround reviews, and in-person sessions when schedules align. The relationships are close, productive, and lasting.
